Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

What We Collect

CalcHub is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage: Your calculation history and theme preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or any identifying information
  • No Tracking: We don't use cookies or tracking scripts (except optional analytics if you consent)

How We Use Data

Any data stored is kept on your device and used only to:

  • Remember your calculation history
  • Save your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Save your language preference

Third-Party Services

We use the following external services:

  • Frankfurter API: For real-time currency exchange rates (no data sent except currency codes)
  • Font Awesome & Google Fonts: For icons and typography (standard web requests)
  • Chart.js: For visualization (runs locally in your browser)

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear your history anytime using the "Clear All" button
  • Your browser controls all stored data
  • No account needed means no data to delete from our servers

Data Security

Since everything runs in your browser, your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (like downloading a PDF).

Children's Privacy

CalcHub is safe for all ages. We don't collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page if our privacy practices change. Check back occasionally.
